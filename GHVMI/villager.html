Features and Further details

There are not much you need to be aware. Just come to my island and talk to the villager. One thing you need to know is that sometimes if you talk to the villager more than once, they would indicate they cannot move to your island because your island is full. But don't panic, they actually already moved to you island, if you go back to your island, their name will be on the plot. Even if there is any problem, I will definately work it out with you.

Usually I can guide you to the villager's house, sometimes when I got busy, I might not be able to lead you. But if you do need I lead you to the correct house, please mention it in the message. Otherwise, you can feel free to go by yourself (it doesn't matter whither you talk to villager first or pick Golds/NMTs first, and it also doesn't matter which way you choose to leave the island).

Golds and NMTs are bonus items, I can’t exchange those to another items if you are not interested in them. But I might put some other stuffs occasionally next to the airport entrance, feel free to pick them too :)

Self-Service Tips:

  1. After you arrive, press ZL button open Nook phone menu -> open map -> move the cursor to the villager, then you will know which house you are going.
  2. Before entering the house, optionally, you can check villager's name by pressing A button in front of their sign(left side of the house)
  3. Please feel free to take Golds and NMTs as many as you like. (You can also do so before you talk to the villagers) If your bag is full and want to threw away something, I got a garbage can for you just next to Golds.

Additional Information

Friendly reminder: You will need an active Nintendo Switch Online subscription service to visit other people's island. And you can also check your console connection by go to [Setting -> Internet -> Test Connection] if the NAT Type shows A or B, you will be alright. If it is C or D, you probably need to fix your Internet connection first.
