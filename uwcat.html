Features and Further details

A-Z Epic Catalog Island, Easy to find, Every furniture with every color, cataloging or looting

Includes ALL 1800+ Catalog-able furniture, 144 wallpapers, 108 floorings, 110 rugs, 95 KK music, indoor, outdoor, all color variations with their corresponding display, anything you can buy back from the Nook Shopping of the ATM machine. Those items are ALL in ALPHABETIC order, check the map posted in the pictures. Bonus items include Nook Miles Furniture, customization kits, bells. (Nook mile furniture is not catalogable, you have to take them home).

Also included ten villagers in box, talk to anyone you like while you looting on the island. (Raymond, Judy, Cyd, Dom, Marshal, Sherb, Audie, Megan, Reneigh, Stitches. You need to have an empty plot)

  • A-Z Furniture:The main furniture section, are all catalog-able (will explain catalog later), and in ALPHABETIC order. Each furniture has all color variations with corresponding display.
  • All Wallpaper/Flooring: Including all purchaseable wallpapers and floorings (Not including Sahara's wallpapers/floorings which are not catalog-able)
  • All Rugs: All purchasable rugs are from Sahara since this is the only way you can buy rugs in the game.
  • Bonus Items: Nook Mile Furniture, bells, customization kits
  • Additional Notes: The game tracks the various type of items in your catalog when you pick them, you need to use Nook Shopping (in ATM machine or NookPhone) to access the catalog. Some items marked NOT FOR SALE also can appear in your catalog. The term Catalog-able I used here basically means if you can buy back from your Nook Shopping catalog.

Once you picked catalog-able items up, you will immediately have the ability to buy them back from Nook Shopping. So You don’t have to put them back one by one, un-wanted items can be tossed in the trash bin. You can choose to catalog them on my island or just loot them, whatever you think suits you best.

Additional Information

Friendly reminder: You will need an active Nintendo Switch Online subscription service to visit other people's island. And you can also check your console connection by go to [Setting -> Internet -> Test Connection] if the NAT Type shows A or B, you will be alright. If it is C or D, you probably need to fix your Internet connection first.

I always prepare the best network environment for you, but we all may experience interruptions at some point. Please don't panic, especially if you are not on the flight (leaving or joining), the gate is often still open and you can use the same Dodo code to come back. If you are experiencing disconnection while you on the flight, there is a high chance the whole connection will be lost. I will try to re-open the gate and give you a new Dodo Code ASAP.
