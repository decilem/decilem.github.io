Villagers Move In.

Choose from all 397 villagers, pick anyone, ready in as soon as a few minutes

Villagers Move In

After you placed the order, please contact me for your preferred time to come. Usually respond quickly, I am in US Eastern Time (New York Time Zone), typically online between 09:00 AM - 11:59 PM EST.

You can choose ANY villager in ACNH. And free feel to ask me any questions if you are not sure how this works. I usually will ask you a few questions to make sure the process will work.

Photos

Features and Further details

Remember to empty your pocket before coming, there are some Golds and Nook Mile Tickets outside the airport, you can fill your pocket with them.

One thing you might need to know is that sometimes if you talk to the villager more than once, they would indicate they cannot move to your island because your island is full. But don't panic, they actually already moved to you island, if you go back to your island, their name will be on the plot. Even if there is any problem, I will definately work it out with you.

Golds and NMTs are bonus items, they cannot be exchanged to another items if you are not interested.

If you never invited villagers from other people, PLEASE READ:

Please remember to have an empty plot on your island to welcome the new villager. The plot will look like the picture show below. There are only two conditions that you will have an empty plot:

  1. After a villager moved out, the day after their packing day, their house will become an empty plot of land for 1 day. (most common)
  2. In your very early game stage, after the first campsite villager, starting from the 7th villager, you can purchase plots of land directly from Tom Nook. But remember, you can only do so after you have 6 villagers on your island.
